Sadakat — Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 11, 2025

Sadakat ("we", "our", or "the App") is a donation and community app for mosque donations, blood requests, and public posts. This Privacy Policy explains what (if any) personal data we request or handle, why we request permissions, and how we protect your privacy.

Summary — short and clear

Sadakat does not store personal data or location data on our servers. The app only requests location permission to show nearby mosques on the map while you are using the map. When you return from the map view, your location is not saved or retained by the app.

What information the app accesses

Location permission: When you open the Map feature to find nearby mosques, Sadakat requests access to your device's location (precise or approximate, depending on your device settings). This permission is used only to show the mosques closest to you in real time while the map is open.

Other data: Sadakat does not require sign-in, and the app does not collect or store personal identifiers, contact lists, messages, or any other personal data on our servers. Public posts and donation actions occur in the app but are not stored by Sadakat (confirm your backend or third-party services if you later connect any server storage).

How we use location data

  • We use your device location only to compute  nearby mosques while you are actively using the map feature.
  • We do not transmit or save that location to our servers after you leave the map view.
  • We do not build user profiles, track movement over time, or sell/ share location data to third parties.

Permissions & your control

You remain in control of location permission. You can grant or revoke location access via your device settings. If you decline permission, the map will not be able to show mosques based on your current location, but other parts of the app will continue to work.

Retention and storage

Sadakat does not store location or personal data on our servers. If you connect the app to any third-party service (for example, to process donations or post content), that is outside the scope of this policy — check the third party's privacy policy for their data practices.

Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect any minimal app data and prevent unauthorized access. Because Sadakat does not store personal/location data on our servers, risks tied to server-side retention are intentionally minimized.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal data to the App, contact us (see below).

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. When we update, we will revise the "Effective date" at the top. You should check this page periodically.
