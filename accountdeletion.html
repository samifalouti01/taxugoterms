How to Delete Your TaxuGo Account

  1. Open the TaxuGo app on your device.
  2. Sign in using your credentials.
  3. Tap the top-right menu .
  4. Select Profile from the dropdown menu.
  5. Scroll down to the section labeled Danger Zone.
  6. Tap Delete Account and follow the confirmation steps.
Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
